On a Sweet Adventure?

We're in.

Fig 1. The Playbook Adventure Socks

We don’t know if student adventures are essential for a free and democratic society, but boy do we love them. That’s why Playbook’s been supporting student adventures, expeditions, and explorations around the world since 2014.

Gone on your own road trip, backpacking trip, or cross-country adventure in the past year? We want in.

Tell us about your experience and what you learned about yourself in ~300 words, submit a photo, and get some adventure socks. Because whether you find yourself in a tuk-tuk, a tent, or a corporate office in the near future, we want to celebrate that spirit of exploration and well, you’re going to need socks, too.
